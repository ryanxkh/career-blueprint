Lead the full product marketing function (team of 4-6) at a Series B or C company, owning positioning, competitive intelligence, and product launch strategy. Be recognized as a go-to voice in the developer tools GTM space.

**5-Year Goal:**
Step into a VP of Marketing role with P&L influence, combining product marketing expertise with demand generation and brand strategy. Help scale a company from $20M to $100M ARR.

**10-15 Year Goal:**
Serve as CMO at a developer-focused company that I genuinely believe in, shaping how technical products are brought to market and building a marketing org that engineers actually respect.

**Ultimate Goal:**
Change how B2B companies communicate with technical buyers. Replace the jargon-heavy, feature-dumping playbook with honest, evidence-based marketing that helps people make better decisions. Leave the field better than I found it.
